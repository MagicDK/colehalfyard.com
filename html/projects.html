Matching Game

Matching Game allows you to put your memory to the test. After clicking play you will be presented with a board of tiles, you have until the timer on the bottom is full to memorize the positions of each of the tiles. After the timer is up the tiles will be hidden and you will have to correctly match pairs of tiles, by matching all tiles on the board you will advance to the next level, match 5 tiles incorrectly and it's game over. How far can you get?
